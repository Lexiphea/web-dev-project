Our Mission

Our mission is to create plushies that inspire joy,
provide comfort, and spark meaningful connections.
We're dedicated to crafting high-quality, lovable
companions that bring warmth and happiness to
people of all ages, one cuddle at a time.

Our Story

Kuddles started with a simple idea: to create
plushies that bring comfort and joy to everyone.
What began as a handmade gift in a small
Maplewood studio has grown into a passion for
crafting huggable companions filled with love. Every
Kuddles plushie is designed to be more than just a
toy — it's a source of warmth, smiles, and lasting
memories.

Our Service

We specialize in crafting high-quality, handmade
plushies that are perfect for every occasion.
Whether you're looking for a thoughtful gift, a
custom-designed plushie, or a cozy companion,
we've got you covered. We also offer
personalization options to make your plushie truly
one-of-a-kind. Whatever your needs, Kuddles is
here to deliver comfort and joy, one hug at a time.
